Museum Game | DUE 11/10

▶️ IN UNITY: Create your own digital museum!

A digital museum is a flexible format. It can be realistic, abstract, fantastical, or metaphorical. It can exhibit real thins, personal things, anything that you'd like. Exhibits can be anything, 3D or 2D.

What makes it a museum is that:

  • You are curating its contents. The works or objects included are linked through your decision to include them together.
  • It's an experience that invites visitors in to see the contents. All museums are essentially very expensive zines that you can walk around in.
  • It's a space visitors decide their route through. Guests form meaning based on how you present a museum's contents. You can guide that experience, but it is ultimately the player's.

Requirements:

This will be a 3D space, navigable in first person (we'll be learning how to do that)

Your museum must include an entry area (somewhere not in the museum where the experience starts), and at least 5 Galleries with exhibits inside

There should be many things to see, read and interpret

Care given to the museum's lighting and sound

The whole museum should take at least 10 minutes to visit

Upload your working build on Itch.io, and submit it on Canvas
