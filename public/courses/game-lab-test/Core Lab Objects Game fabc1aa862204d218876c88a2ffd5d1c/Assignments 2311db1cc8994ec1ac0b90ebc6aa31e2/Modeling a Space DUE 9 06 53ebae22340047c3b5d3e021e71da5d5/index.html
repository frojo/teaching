Modeling a Space | DUE 9/06

Let's get our feet wet in using Unity!

▶️ IN UNITY: **re-create a space from your daily life** (your apartment, including the door? a yard? a basement?) using 3D primitives. **No input or gameplay allowed**, just make a somewhat complex static 3D scene.

  • place at least ~150 objects (cubes, cylinders, spheres, etc.) AND/OR spend at least 2-3 hours on building the scene (hint: remember to use Ctrl+D / Cmd+D to Duplicate objects!)

  • build out to WebGL, upload to itch.io (we'll go over how to do this on Friday), and submit it on Canvas
