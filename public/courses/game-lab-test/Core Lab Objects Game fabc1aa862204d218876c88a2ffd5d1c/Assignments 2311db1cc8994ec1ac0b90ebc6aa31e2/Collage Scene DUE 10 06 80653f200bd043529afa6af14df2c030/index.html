Collage Scene | DUE 10/06

▶️ IN UNITY: This week, edit some found assets together into an animated collage!
  • All your assets must come from a public domain collection, do not use google image search. Some places to get started:
    • NYPL Digital Collections
    • The Met's Open Access collections
    • Flickr's The Commons project
    • The Library of Congress's digital collections
    • Or take a look through The Public Domain Review or Open Culture
  • You can edit and embellish the assets you find in Krita, but don't add too much of your own original illustration

Just what is it that makes today's homes so different, so appealing?, Richard Hamilton, 1956

Cutting up existing art to create new work through their juxtaposition is a longstanding technique of modern art!

Think about the original pieces and the context of their creation, and how your new assemblage changes that meaning.
